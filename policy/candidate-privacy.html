1. Scope

This notice applies to all personal data collected from job applicants ("Candidates") at Untitled Needleworks. This is separate from our main Privacy Policy, which covers website users and customers.

2. Data We Collect

When you apply for a role, we collect data necessary to evaluate your application. This includes:

  • Contact Information: Full name, email address, contact number.
  • Professional History: Resume/CV, education details, certifications, employment history, portfolio links (e.g., LinkedIn).
  • Application Details: Preferred role, statement of interest, availability, referral information, and any other information you voluntarily provide.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for recruitment purposes, including:

  • Assessing your skills, qualifications, and suitability for the role.
  • Communicating with you about the application process and your application status.
  • Verifying your information and conducting reference checks (with your prior consent).
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Access is strictly limited to the internal hiring team and managers involved in the interview process. We only use secure, third-party services (like email or applicant tracking systems) that are bound by confidentiality and data protection agreements.

5. Data Retention

If your application is successful, your data will become part of your employee file.

If your application is unsuccessful, we will retain your data for 12 months. We do this to consider you for future suitable openings, unless you request that we delete your data sooner.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time. For requests or questions, please contact us.

7. Contact

For any questions about this notice, please contact legal@untitledneedleworks.com or careers@untitledneedleworks.com.
