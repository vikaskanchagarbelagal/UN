1. Commitment

We aim to make our sites and apps usable by everyone, including people with disabilities.

2. Standards & Practices

  • WCAG 2.1 AA targets; semantic HTML; keyboard navigation; ARIA where needed.
  • Color-contrast and reduced-motion preferences respected.

3. Feedback

Report accessibility issues to access@untitledneedleworks.com . We review and address promptly.

4. Compatibility

Works with current versions of major browsers and assistive technologies. Some older systems may not be fully supported.
