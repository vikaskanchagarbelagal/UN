Company Policy

Effective Date: 18-11-2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Untitled Needleworks. These Company Policies outline the framework, ethical standards, and operational rules that govern our business. By engaging with our company—whether as an employee, partner, or user—you agree to adhere to these policies.

2. Code of Conduct

  • Employees must uphold professionalism, integrity, and transparency.
  • No tolerance for harassment, discrimination, or unethical practices.
  • Confidential company information must not be disclosed without authorization.

3. Employment Policies

Equal Opportunity

We are committed to an inclusive and diverse workplace. Employment decisions are based solely on qualifications and merit.

Workplace Behavior

Employees must maintain respect, professionalism, and collaborative spirit at all times.

Remote & Hybrid Work

Work-from-home and hybrid models are permitted based on project needs, subject to manager approval.

4. Data Security & Privacy

We strictly follow international data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, etc.). Employees and contractors handling sensitive information must ensure encryption, limited access, and secure communication.

5. Use of Technology

Company resources including laptops, software, and servers must be used responsibly. Unauthorized installations, piracy, or tampering with systems is prohibited.

6. Intellectual Property

All designs, code, research, and creative works produced under Untitled Needleworks remain company property unless explicitly stated otherwise.

7. Confidentiality

Employees and partners must protect company secrets, designs, client data, and trade practices both during and after engagement with the company.

8. Incident Reporting

Any suspected breaches, ethical concerns, or workplace incidents must be immediately reported to HR or the Admin Panel team.

9. Compliance

Employees and partners are expected to comply with global laws including labor laws, intellectual property rights, and anti-bribery legislation.

10. Enforcement

Violations of these policies may result in disciplinary actions including warnings, suspension, or termination.

11. Updates

These policies are subject to change. Updates will be communicated via official company channels.
