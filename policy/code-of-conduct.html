1. Introduction & Scope

This Code of Conduct outlines the ethical standards we uphold at Untitled Needleworks. It applies to all our employees, partners, contractors, and community members in all interactions related to our business, including online forums, support channels, and in-person events.

2. Our Community Standards

We are building a global community centered on creativity and technology. We expect all participants to be:

  • Respectful: We do not tolerate harassment, discrimination, or abusive language in any form. Disagreements are normal; disrespect is not.
  • Constructive: We encourage feedback and collaboration. Aim to be helpful, not harmful.
  • Inclusive: We welcome people of all backgrounds, identities, and abilities.

3. Our Business Ethics

Untitled Needleworks is committed to conducting business with integrity:

  • Anti-Bribery & Corruption: We have a zero tolerance policy for bribery, kickbacks, or any form of corruption.
  • Fair Competition: We compete fairly and honestly in the marketplace.
  • Customer Trust: We are truthful in our marketing and transparent about our products and data practices.
  • Confidentiality: We protect the confidential information entrusted to us by our customers, partners, and employees.

4. Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience a violation of this Code, we urge you to report it. You can contact our team at ethics@untitledneedleworks.com.

All reports will be treated confidentially and investigated promptly. We are committed to protecting against retaliation for any good-faith report.
