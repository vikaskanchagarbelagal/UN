2. Our Hiring Philosophy

Talent is everywhere, but opportunity is not. We are actively working to create an equitable and inclusive hiring process. This includes:

  • Writing job descriptions that are inclusive and free of biased language.
  • Committing to a structured, skills-based interview process.
  • Actively sourcing talent from under represented communities.
  • Valuing non-traditional backgrounds and self-taught skills.
This is why our careers page invites you to apply even if you don't see an open role. We believe in your skills and talent, not just a predefined checklist.

3. Our Culture

We are dedicated to making inclusion a daily practice. This means:

  • Promoting flexible work arrangements to support different life needs.
  • Ensuring our digital and physical spaces are accessible.
  • Encouraging open dialogue and providing channels for feedback.
  • Investing in continuous learning for our team on topics of bias and allyship.

4. Accountability

This is a journey, not a destination. We hold ourselves accountable for making progress and will remain transparent in our efforts. We welcome feedback from our team and our community on how we can improve.
